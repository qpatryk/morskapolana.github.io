About us

Morska Polana is a new and dynamically developing complex
of year-round holiday houses located in Mielno,
a popular holiday resort,
famous for its beautiful and well-kept Baltic beach,
right next to the seaside spit and Lake Jamno

For Who

Morska Polana was created for whole families
- both young and older generations
- who appreciate unrestricted relaxation
in a charming green environment
and spacious and comfortable houses.
It is a place created for people
looking for a respite from the hardships
of everyday life and the city noise.

What can we offer

We offer our guests nearly 2 hectares of green space,
surrounded by comfortable and insulated year-round houses
with their own barbecue area,
a beach volleyball court made of sea sand,
a trampoline with a diameter of 5 meters and a playground for children.
From year to year Morska Polana
and its recreational infrastructure develop and improve,
so that guests visiting the resort will be satisfied
with their stay and will come back to us in the future.

Our location

Our houses are located in a quiet and peaceful part of Mielno,
about a 15-minute walk from the wide and sandy beach,
which can be reached via a residential road
or by the embankment along the picturesque Jamno Lake,
leading to the marinas and numerous tourist attractions.
